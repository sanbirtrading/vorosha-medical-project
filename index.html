Objectives Are:

1) Our main objective is to make professional counseling available to everyone whether they are in home, office or even in out of Dhaka in a remote location.

2) We want to provide the best service at a very minimum price offer.

3) We want to break the taboo of people speaking against any type of mental health related problems.

4) We want to let people understand this is not a disease and it can be cured based on time through proper direction and self- care and self-love.

Mission Are:

1)We want to raise awareness regarding psychological problems among the public and we want people not to think this psychological problems as taboo in the society.

2)We want people to understand their problems first and then to work on that for their betterment.

3) We want to help our clients in such positive and friendly way that they start believing in themselves and start leading a productive, healthy and happy life within a very short time.

4) We want our clients to develop the power of confidence, self-esteem and to let them understand their strength which will help them to be successful and also we want to help them to get rid of trauma, anxiety and stress related problems.

5) Stigma is a huge factor in holding people back from seeking mental health so we want to eliminate the stigma surrounded by them.

Vission Are:

Our vision is to build a happy, healthy and positive world for all by applying professional skillful approach.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What you can expect from a Psychologist?

    You can expect a psychologist to understand your situation and background. He / she will also ask you what prompted you to seek treatment. The psychologist will ask you about your current life circumstances and past treatment scenario if you have taken any and then they will plan to guide you through different treatment approaches which will help you in the long run to overcome and resolve problems if you actually follow and practice suggestions from your psychologist in your day to day life.

  • What you can expect from a Psychiatrist?

    Psychiatrist is a medical doctor that specializes in mental health and illness. They can prescribe medicine if they feel it is alright for you for your well-being. As they are medical doctors so psychiatrist can prescribe the medicine that they think can benefit client’s mental health. They will mostly focus on the medical aspects of the mental health.

  • What you can expect from a therapist?

    You can expect a therapist will work with you to develop goals. He/ she will support you and help you and also will challenge you when you are ready to be challenged. Your therapist will help you to open up with him with problems and also will guide you to a long time result oriented goal and you will need to achieve it and above all you need to practice things that are been said you to be practice during your sessions.
